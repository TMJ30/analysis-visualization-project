# U.S. Metropolitan Real Estate Analysis 

## Overview 
This project is a collaborative group analysis focused on real estate market trends across major metroploitan areaas in the United States. Using Python-based data analysis and visualization techniques, the team aimed to identify meaningful patterns, relationships, and regional differences withing urban housing markets, and to communicate those insights through clear and effective visualizations. 

## Structure & Contributions
This was a group project where each member contributed to different aspects of the analysis. My individual contributions can be found in the [Toni J](https://github.com/TMJ30/analysis-visualization-project/tree/main/Toni%20J) folder within this repository. This section highlughts my work in data transformation & analysis, visualizations, and interpretations of results related to the project's research questions.

## Visualizations
A series of visualizations were developed to explore key research questions with multiple charts supporting each area of analysis. Each visualization is clearly and accurately labeled to ensure readability and understanding. These visuals are accompanieed by detailed explanations that highlight important trends, including housing prices, market fluctuations over time, and differences across metroplitaiton regions
